## FIELD LABELS AND PLACEHOLDER TEXT

### Business Name

**Label:** Your business name

**Placeholder:** e.g. Jaco Venter Plumbing

**Helper text:** Use the name you want to appear on your website and in Google search results. If you trade under your own name, use that.

---

### Business Category / Trade

**Label:** What type of business is this?

**Placeholder:** e.g. Plumber, Therapist, Accountant, Electrician

**Helper text:** Be specific. "Plumber" ranks better than "home services." Google uses this to decide which searches your site is relevant for.

---

### Primary Suburb / Location

**Label:** Where are you based?

**Placeholder:** e.g. Centurion, Cape Town Southern Suburbs, Sandton

**Helper text:** This becomes your primary location in your schema markup. If you serve a wider area, you can list additional suburbs on the next screen.

---

### Service Area

**Label:** Which suburbs or areas do you serve?

**Placeholder:** e.g. Centurion, Midrand, Pretoria East, Pretoria CBD

**Helper text:** You can list up to five areas. Each one adds a local search signal to your site. If you serve the whole city, just write the city name.

---

### Services Offered

**Label:** What services do you offer?

**Placeholder:** List your main services, one per line. e.g. Geyser replacement, Drain unblocking, Emergency plumbing

**Helper text:** Each service you list here will get its own section on your site. If you offer a service you want to be found for on Google, it needs to be named here explicitly.

---

### Owner / Contact Name

**Label:** Your name (as it will appear on the site)

**Placeholder:** e.g. Ruan Botha

**Helper text:** For sole traders and personal practices, using your full name builds trust with potential clients who are researching you before they call.

---

### Phone Number

**Label:** Your South African mobile number

**Placeholder:** e.g. +27 82 000 0000

**Helper text:** This is the number your enquiry form will route to via WhatsApp. Make sure it is a WhatsApp-enabled number that you check regularly.

---

### Email Address

**Label:** Your email address

**Placeholder:** e.g. yourname@gmail.com

**Helper text:** This is where your login details, backup confirmations, and occasional maintenance notices go. Use an address you actually check.

---

### Preferred Domain Name

**Label:** What domain name would you like?

**Placeholder:** e.g. jacoventerplumbing.co.za or jacoplumber.co.za

**Helper text:** We will check availability for you. If your first choice is taken, we will suggest alternatives. A .co.za domain is R150 per year and is included in your R1,999.

---

### Operating Hours

**Label:** Your operating hours

**Placeholder:** e.g. Monday to Friday 8am to 5pm, Saturday 8am to 1pm

**Helper text:** This goes into your schema markup so Google can show your hours in search results. If your hours vary, write what is most accurate for most weeks.

---

### Photo Upload (Optional)

**Label:** A professional photo of you or your business (optional, but recommended)

**Placeholder:** (file upload field)

**Helper text:** A real photo of you or your team makes an enormous difference in personal-trust industries. A high-quality phone photo is fine. If you do not have one yet, we can use professional placeholders for the initial build and you can add your own within the first 30 days.

---

### Logo Upload (Optional)

**Label:** Your logo, if you have one (optional)

**Placeholder:** (file upload field)

**Helper text:** PNG or SVG format preferred. If you do not have a logo yet, do not let it delay your site. We will note this and use clean text branding until you have one. Most clients get the site live first and the logo sorted shortly after.

---

### Additional Notes

**Label:** Anything else I should know?

**Placeholder:** e.g. I have specific wording I want used for one of my services. I already have a domain I would like to use. My previous website had content I want to reference.

**Helper text:** This is not a formal brief. Just tell me anything that would help me build the right site for you. Even a single sentence is useful.
