Yes. WordPress uses the Gutenberg block editor for content, which most business owners can learn for basic updates within an hour. For local search in South Africa, this foundation, combined with an active Google Business profile and consistent review acquisition, is sufficient to produce meaningful organic ranking improvements without ongoing monthly SEO spend.

**Q10: Why WordPress instead of Wix or Squarespace?**

Three reasons. First, WordPress sites score 88 to 95 on Google PageSpeed Mobile. Wix sites score 35 to 55. That difference directly affects Google rankings. Second, WordPress is open-source software that you own and can take anywhere. Wix is a proprietary platform you rent. Third, WordPress gives you administrative access to your site and your data. Wix exports are limited. Your investment on a WordPress site is an asset. Your investment on Wix is a subscription.
